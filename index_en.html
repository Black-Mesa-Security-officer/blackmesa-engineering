Division Overview

The Engineering & Manufacturing Division supports scientific progress through high-precision fabrication and mechanical design. From nuclear waste handling systems to portal-capable power suits, this division builds the tools that make research at Black Mesa possible.

Core Projects

  • HEV Mk I–V Power Suit Series
  • Mass Spectrometers & Anti-Mass Spectrometer
  • Radiation-hardened Sample Containment Pods
  • Autonomous Loader Systems & Robotic Armatures

HEV Systems

Each HEV unit integrates remote vitals monitoring, kinetic armor plating, and modular field-assist tools. The Mk IV model remains the operational standard with full HUD integration and ELM connectivity.

Manufacturing Standards

All engineers wear orange jumpsuits or supervisor-designated uniforms. Safety gear, such as helmets and harnesses, is mandatory in all active zones. Fabrication procedures follow internal ISO-class precision standards and are overseen by division foremen.
